Our Background

With the popularity of Cryptocurrency and digital currency platforms, for example: PayPal, Perfect money and Skrill etc. Proxchange has stepped in to aid in closing that gap. We have established ourselves as a trusted partner for individuals and businesses seeking reliable and efficient payment solutions.

Our Mission

Proxchange exists to proffer reliable, long term solutions to cross boundary finantial limitations of freelancers, families, friends, employees and contractors in accessing financial services, remunerations, gifts, wages, benefits, etc. As well as making payments or transfers accordingly.

Our Vision

We seek to provide a viable platform that corrects the cross boundary financial limitations faced by entrepreneurs, small businesses, freelancers, families, friends, employees and contractors in sending and receiving money in foreign currencies leveraging on digital currencies and cryptocurrency tech systems.

Our Values

Our core values at Proxchange remain: Trust, Reliability, Growth, Innovation and Positivity.
